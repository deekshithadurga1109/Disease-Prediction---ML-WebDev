About Us

We are a passionate team of four students from MVGR College of Engineering, working on Health Disease Prediction System as our mini-project.
Our goal is to create an innovative and user-friendly platform that helps in early disease detection using machine learning models.

Our project aims to assist individuals and healthcare professionals in making data-driven health decisions by analyzing symptoms and medical data.

We believe technology can revolutionize healthcare, and we are excited to contribute to this vision!
